# Recreate

Recreate is a desktop app for producing videos end to end — write a script, generate a
voiceover, gather images and stock footage, and assemble the final video, all from a
single workflow.

**Free to download and use.** Available for **Windows** (x64 and arm64) and **macOS**
(Apple Silicon).

## Features

- **Script writing** — draft and refine video scripts with AI assistance.
- **Voiceover** — text-to-speech narration with selectable voices, languages, and pacing.
- **Image generation** — produce images for your video; generation keeps running in the
  background even when the app is unfocused.
- **Stock media library** — search and pull in footage and photos from Pexels, Pixabay,
  and Unsplash without leaving the app.
- **Built-in browser** — find and bring in references directly inside Recreate.
- **Video assembly & export** — combine narration, images, and footage into a finished
  video.
- **Customizable prompts** — tune the behavior of each step to fit your workflow.
- **Automatic updates** — the app detects new releases and offers to update.

## Download & install

Get the latest build from the [releases page](https://github.com/ammarrik/recreate-app/releases).

**Windows** — run the installer for your architecture:

- `Recreate-Setup-<version>-x64.exe` — Intel/AMD (64-bit)
- `Recreate-Setup-<version>-arm64.exe` — ARM (e.g. Snapdragon)

Windows may show a SmartScreen warning since the installer isn't code-signed yet — choose
**More info → Run anyway**.

**macOS (Apple Silicon)** — open `Recreate-<version>-arm64.dmg` and drag **Recreate** into
**Applications**.

Because the app isn't notarized yet, macOS may block the first launch — right-click
**Recreate** in Applications, choose **Open**, then confirm.

## Updating

Already running Recreate? It checks for newer releases automatically and offers to update —
no manual download needed.
